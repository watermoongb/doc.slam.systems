**PLAY OVERLAY**

Tap OVERLAY in playlist bar

Tap thumbnail button to play overlay clip

**Cancel Overlay**

Tap the currently playing Overlay clip
![Step2](Overlay-Tap-Cancel.jpg)


Overlay is remove from normal clip
